Hope Park is a semi-autobiographical portrayal of depression, told from the standpoint of a nameless, faceless individual. This protagonist's subjective reality is bared poignantly through a fractured narrative and minimal artwork. Printed on transparent pages, elements interact and bleed into one another- calling for the reader to dissect and explore.

Book One collects the first three chapters of Hope Park. The first chapter is available for online preview here, while digital (pdf format) or physical copies of the first book can be purchased below.

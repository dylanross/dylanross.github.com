TEACHING

I teach on the following undergraduate courses :

Cells to Organisms (CTO) -- I maintain an online simulation of cell proliferation and differentiation, which students use in a ``dry'' practical. The students have to experiment with different division, death, and differentiation rates to produce robust populations of each cell type in appropriate proportions.

System Design Project (SDP) -- I mentor a team of ~6 students as they build a LEGO robot capable of playing football. The students build the physical robot from scratch, and also have to write their own sensing, planning, and control code.

Intelligent Autonomous Robotics (IAR) -- I help my supervisor, Barbara Webb, run a series of practicals in which students must program a Khepera robot to perform tasks autonomously (i.e. without human intervention). Tasks include avoiding obstacles, performing odometry / dead reckoning, and collecting and returning ``food'' to a ``nest''.
